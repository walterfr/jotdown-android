Jotdown is a complete academic reader that works 100% offline on your device.

Features:
- Integrated PDF reader with zoom and page navigation
- Highlight and capture citations directly from the PDF
- Add sticky notes and annotations anywhere on the page
- Draw and highlight with pen, pencil and highlighter tools
- Export your notes in .MD, .TXT or .PDF in ABNT format
- All data is saved locally via IndexedDB — no cloud, no account required

Perfect for students, researchers and teachers who need to organize academic readings efficiently.
